Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

The Globe Network ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you participate in our Emergency Response: Liberty County (ERLC) roleplay servers and use our associated Discord services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Discord username and ID
  • Roblox username
  • Communication data within our Discord server and ERLC servers
  • Server activity logs
  • Application form responses

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Managing and operating our ERLC servers
  • Processing applications
  • Enforcing our server rules and guidelines
  • Investigating reported incidents
  • Communicating with users about server updates and events
  • Improving our services and user experience

Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Your information is only accessible to authorized staff members for server management purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Access to user data is restricted to authorized staff members only, and we regularly review our security practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

Children's Privacy

While we welcome players of all ages in our ERLC servers, our Discord server is strictly for users aged 13 and older, in compliance with Discord's Terms of Service. We do not knowingly allow or collect information from children under 13 in our Discord server.

If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child under 13 has accessed our Discord server, please contact us immediately through Discord or email. We will take steps to remove their access and any associated information.

For players under 13:

  • You are welcome to play on our ERLC servers
  • You must not access our Discord server until you are 13
  • Parental supervision is recommended
  • You must follow all server rules and guidelines

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through our Discord server and website.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our Discord server or email.
