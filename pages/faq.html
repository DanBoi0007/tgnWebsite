What are the requirements to become staff?

Staff requirements include being at least 14 years old, being in our Discord Server, having a clear microphone, speaking fluent English, being atleast Network Level 10, and maintaining a professional attitude. Check our application page for full details.

How do I report a player?

To report a player, use the /ticket command in our Discord Server to create a player report ticket. Please provide evidence such as screenshots or recordings when possible.

What are the server rules?

You can find our complete server rules in our Community Guidelines. We maintain a strict but fair environment to ensure everyone can enjoy their roleplay experience.

What can I wear in-game?

Unlike most ERLC servers, we allow a wide range of avatar items and customization options. However, all avatars must still follow our Avatar Guidelines to maintain realism and professionalism. This includes appropriate uniforms for emergency services roles and realistic civilian attire.

Do you have a store page?

Our server does not have a store page, as we believe in maintaining a fair and a non-pay to win environment for all players. While we do accept dsonations to help support the servers, these are purely optional and come with no in-game benefits, perks or advantages.
