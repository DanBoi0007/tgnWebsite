Community Guidelines

Creating a safe and enjoyable environment for all players

1. Respect Others

  • Treat all other players with respect and kindness, both in-character (IC) and out-of-character (OOC). Harassment, bullying and toxic behaviour is strictly prohibited and will lead to swift removal from all of our servers.

2. Stay In-Character

  • When participating in roleplay, always remain in-character unless otherwise specified. If you need to speak out-of-character (OOC), make sure to clearly indicate this by using appropriate tags. For example: (OOC) [Your message]. This helps avoid confusion and keeps the roleplay experience enjoyable for all players.

3. Follow Roblox & Discord's Terms of Service

  • All actions, language and behaviour must comply with Roblox and Discord's rules. You can find Roblox's Community Guidelines here and Discord's TOS here.

4. Follow Staff Directions

  • Network moderators and admins are here to keep the community safe and enjoyable. Always follow their instructions and contact them by using the /ticket command in any channel if you need support.

5. No Exploiting or Cheating

  • The use of exploits, hacks or third-party software is strictly forbidden not only on our network, but on Roblox as a whole. Play fair and keep the experience fun for everyone.

6. No Staff Impersonation

  • Do not pretend or impersonate any of our staff members in any way. This includes using similar names, profile pictures or falsely claiming authority over our servers.

7. No Real-Life Event Recreations

  • Do not recreate or roleplay real-life events, especially those involving tragedies, violence, or sensitive topics. This includes historical or current events that may be deemed disrespectful or inappropriate for the community. Keep roleplay fictional and respectful.

8. No Spamming or Flooding

  • Avoid spamming messages and commands in our channels. Flooding the in-game chat disrupts the roleplay experience for others.

9. Appropriate Character Names and Behaviour

  • All in-game character names, Discord usernames, behaviour and backstories must be appropriate for our servers. Avoid using offensive, inappropriate or troll names.

10. No Meta-Gaming

  • Do not use out-of-character (OOC) information to influence in-character (IC) discussions and actions. Keep IC and OOC knowledge separate to maintain fairness in roleplay scenes.

11. No Advertising

  • Advertising other servers, games or groups is strictly prohibited. The # self-promotion channel may only be used to share YouTube, Twitch, or other media content related to our servers. To post you must have the @Content Creator role.

12. Avatar Guidelines

  • When roleplaying on our servers, we allow you to wear a big range of items, not typically allowed on most servers. All we ask is you read and follow our Avatar Guidelines. If you have any questions about this, feel free to ask a staff member!

Questions and Support

If you have questions about these guidelines or need to report a violation, please:

  • Contact a staff member in-game
  • Use the /ticket command in our Discord server
  • Visit our Contact page

Important Note: These guidelines are subject to change. Staff members have final say in all situations and may take appropriate action as needed to maintain a positive community environment.
