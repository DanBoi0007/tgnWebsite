About The Globe Network

Building a global community of ERLC roleplayers

Our Story

The Globe Network was founded with a vision to create an inclusive, international community for Emergency Response: Liberty County players. We believe that roleplay experiences should transcend geographical boundaries and bring together players from different cultures and backgrounds.

Starting with our first servers in London, Rio de Janeiro, and Giza, we are committed to expanding our network, each new server delivering immersive roleplay and offering unique roleplay experiences that reflect local emergency response practices and cultures.

Today, we're proud to host a thriving community of dedicated roleplayers, supported by our professional staff team who ensure high-quality, immersive experiences across all our servers.

Our Core Values

Community First

We prioritize creating a welcoming, inclusive environment where every player feels valued and respected.

Quality Roleplay

We maintain high standards for roleplay to ensure immersive and enjoyable experiences for all players.

Cultural Diversity

We celebrate different cultures and perspectives, incorporating them into our unique server experiences.
